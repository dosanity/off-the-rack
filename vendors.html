VENDOR POLICY

We founded Off The Rack with the goals of empowering the younger generation of entrepreneurs in our community and making an impact. We want to fully support those who respect our company's mission and purpose. To our clients, make sure to thoroughly review the guidelines prior to reserving a spot for our pop up events. Let us support and empower one another.

GUIDLINES

Welcome to Off The Rack!


General Information

  • Vendor spaces will be assigned first come basis at load-in.
  • You are provided with a 10x10 space. It is your responsibility to set up/take down, and clean anything that you see fit.
  • Please note that any tents/canopies must be weighted down on all 4 corners in order to be used: failure to do so will result in our team asking you to take it down.
  • Off The Rack does not provide tents, tent weights, tables, chairs, or any supplies.
  • All markets are rain, snow or shine! No refunds available. Make sure to plan in advance.

Vendor Information

  • Load-in from 8-10 a.m.
  • Set up by 10 a.m.
  • Check at 9:30 a.m.
  • Start packing at 4 p.m.
  • Be off campus by 6 p.m.
